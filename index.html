Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions. If you need more help, please contact Campus Security.

How long do items stay on the portal?

Lost items remain active until you mark them as 'found'. Unclaimed found items are automatically cleared from the main view after a few days to keep the listings current, but success stories are kept in a separate section.

What should I do after I find my item?

That's great news! Please click the "I Found This!" button on your original lost item post. This moves it to the "Success Stories" section and helps keep the portal organized for everyone.

Is my email address public?

Yes, the email address you provide is visible so that finders or owners can reach you directly. For your safety, we only accept email addresses, not phone numbers. Please only provide an email you are comfortable sharing with the campus community.

What if no one claims an item I found?

After a reasonable amount of time, we recommend turning the item into the central campus security or administrative office. The system automatically cleans up very old posts to keep the portal focused on active items.
